# Government of Maharashtra (GoM)

- **GoM** stands for **Government of Maharashtra**.
- It is responsible for governing the state through various departments.
- This role falls under the **Planning Department**.

---

# Planning Department

## Purpose
- Plans Maharashtra's economic and social development.
- Uses data and analytics for policy-making.
- Supports district planning and evidence-based decision-making.
- Enables data-driven governance.

---

# MITRA (Maharashtra Institution for Transformation)

## About
- Works under the **Planning Department**.
- Supports government planning through data, technology, and innovation.
- Implements the **MahaSTRIDE Programme**.

---

# MahaSTRIDE Programme

## Objective
- Strengthens district planning, monitoring, and governance.
- Uses technology and data to improve decision-making.
- Supports Maharashtra's vision of becoming a **USD 1 trillion economy by FY 2028**.
