Government of Maharashtra (GoM)
GoM stands for Government of Maharashtra.
It governs the state through various departments.
This role comes under the Planning Department.
Planning Department
Plans Maharashtra's economic and social development.
Uses data for policy making and district planning.
Supports data-driven government decisions.
MITRA (Maharashtra Institution for Transformation)
Works under the Planning Department.
Supports government planning using data and technology.
Implements the MahaSTRIDE Programme.
MahaSTRIDE Programme
Government programme to strengthen district planning and monitoring.
Uses technology and data for better governance.
Supports Maharashtra's goal of becoming a USD 1 trillion economy by FY 2028.
